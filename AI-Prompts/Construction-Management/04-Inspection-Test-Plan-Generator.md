# 🔍 Inspection & Test Plan (ITP) Generator

## Purpose

Generate a complete Inspection and Test Plan (ITP) for any construction activity, ensuring compliance with project specifications, approved drawings, contract requirements, international standards, and quality management systems.

The generated ITP should be suitable for consultant and client approval and support QA/QC inspection processes throughout the project lifecycle.

---

# AI Role

Act as a Senior QA/QC Manager with over 20 years of experience preparing Inspection & Test Plans (ITPs) for EPC, infrastructure, industrial, commercial, residential, and mega construction projects.

You are an expert in:

- Quality Management
- ISO 9001
- Inspection & Testing
- Material Inspection
- Construction Quality Assurance
- Client Quality Requirements
- Consultant Approval Processes

---

Provide:

- Project Name
- Client

- Contractor
- Activity Name
- Work Package
- IFC Drawings
- Approved Shop Drawings
- Project Specifications
- Applicable Standards
- Method Statement
- Material Submittals

# Tasks

## 1. Activity Description

Define:

- Work Scope
- Inspection Scope
- Inspection Objectives

---

## 2. Applicable References

List:

- Project Specifications
- IFC Drawings
- Shop Drawings
- Method Statement
- Material Approval
- Applicable Codes & Standards

---

## 3. Inspection Stages

Identify inspection stages including:

- Material Receiving Inspection
- Pre-Installation Inspection
- In-Process Inspection
- Final Inspection
- Testing
- Handover Inspection

---

## 4. Inspection Requirements

For each stage define:

- Inspection Activity
- Acceptance Criteria
- Responsible Party
- Required Records

---

## 5. Hold / Witness / Review Points

Identify:

- Hold Points (H)
- Witness Points (W)
- Review Points (R)
- Surveillance Points (S)

Explain the purpose of each.

---

## 6. Testing Requirements

Generate required tests including:

- Material Tests
- Laboratory Tests
- Field Tests
- Functional Tests
- Performance Tests

Specify:

- Test Standard
- Frequency
- Acceptance Criteria

---

List:

- Material Certificates
