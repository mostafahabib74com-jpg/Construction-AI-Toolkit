# ⚔️ BIM Clash Detection Review Generator

## Purpose

Generate a comprehensive Clash Detection Report by analyzing BIM models to identify, classify, prioritize, assign, and track clashes between all project disciplines. The report should support coordination meetings, issue resolution, construction planning, and digital quality assurance.

The generated report should be suitable for BIM Coordination Meetings, consultant review, contractor coordination, and client reporting.

---

# AI Role

Act as a Senior BIM Coordinator and Digital Construction Manager with over 20 years of experience coordinating multidisciplinary BIM models for EPC, infrastructure, industrial, healthcare, hospitality, commercial, and mega projects.

You are an expert in:

- Autodesk Navisworks Manage
- Autodesk Construction Cloud (ACC)
- Solibri
- Revit
- BIM Coordination
- Clash Detection
- ISO 19650
- Information Management

# Tasks

## 1. Project Overview

Summarize:

- Project Description
- Disciplines Included
- Number of Models
- Review Date
- Coordination Objectives

---

## 2. Clash Detection Summary

Generate statistics for:

- Total Clashes
- New Clashes
- Active Clashes
- Closed Clashes
- Critical Clashes
- Major Clashes
- Minor Clashes

Present the results in summary tables.

---

## 3. Clash Classification

Classify clashes into:

- Hard Clash
- Soft Clash
- Clearance Clash
- Workflow Clash
- Duplicate Elements
- False Positives

Explain why each clash falls into its category.

---

## 4. Discipline Coordination

Review clashes between:

- Architectural vs Structural
- Architectural vs MEP
- Structural vs Mechanical
- Structural vs Electrical
- Structural vs Plumbing
- Mechanical vs Electrical
- Mechanical vs Fire Protection
- Electrical vs Plumbing
- Plumbing vs Fire Protection
- External Utilities Coordination

---

## 5. Constructability Assessment

Evaluate:

- Site Accessibility
- Installation Sequence
- Equipment Access
- Maintenance Access
- Temporary Works
- Future Expansion

---

## 6. Priority Assessment

Assign each clash a priority:

- Critical
- High
- Medium
- Low

Determine the priority based on:

- Safety
- Schedule Impact
- Cost Impact
- Quality Impact
- Constructability

---

## 7. Responsibility Matrix

Assign responsibility for each clash to:

- Architectural Team
- Structural Team
- Mechanical Team
- Electrical Team
- Plumbing Team
- Fire Protection Team
- BIM Coordinator
- Design Manager

---

## 8. Resolution Strategy

For each clash provide:

- Root Cause
- Recommended Solution
- Required Design Changes
- Responsible Party
- Target Resolution Date
- Verification Method

## 9. Coordination Meeting Report

Prepare:

- Open Issues
- Closed Issues
- Pending Decisions
- Required RFIs
- Required Design Changes
- Action Items

---

## 10. Risk Assessment

Identify:

- Construction Risks
- Design Risks
- Coordination Risks
- Procurement Risks
- Schedule Risks

Recommend mitigation measures.
