# 🎯 BIM Handover & Project Closeout Generator

## Purpose

Generate a comprehensive BIM Handover and Project Closeout Report that validates all digital deliverables before final project handover. The report shall confirm that BIM models, documentation, asset information, COBie datasets, IFC files, and operational data comply with contractual requirements and are ready for owner acceptance and facility management.

The generated report should support Owners, Consultants, Contractors, BIM Managers, Facility Managers, and Asset Managers during the final handover process.

# Tasks

- Contract Completion Date

- Handover Milestones

Verify availability and quality of:
