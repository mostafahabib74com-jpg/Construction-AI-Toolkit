# 📈 KPI Generator

## Purpose

Generate a comprehensive set of Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) to evaluate project performance across schedule, cost, quality, safety, procurement, productivity, and overall project health.

---

## AI Role

Act as a Senior Project Controls Manager with extensive experience in construction project performance measurement, Primavera P6, Cost Control, Earned Value Management (EVM), and executive reporting.

---

# Required Inputs

Provide:

- Project Name
- Reporting Period
- Planned Progress
- Actual Progress
- Budget
- Actual Cost
- Earned Value
- Planned Value
- Resource Data
- Procurement Status
- Safety Statistics
- Quality Statistics
- Risk Register

---

# Tasks

## 1. Schedule KPIs

Calculate:

- Planned Progress (%)
- Actual Progress (%)
- Schedule Variance (SV)
- Schedule Performance Index (SPI)
- Milestone Achievement Rate
- Critical Activity Completion Rate

---

## 2. Cost KPIs

Calculate:

- Budget at Completion (BAC)
- Actual Cost (AC)
- Earned Value (EV)
- Cost Variance (CV)
- Cost Performance Index (CPI)
- Estimate at Completion (EAC)
- Estimate to Complete (ETC)

---

## 3. Productivity KPIs

Measure:

- Labor Productivity
- Equipment Utilization
- Crew Efficiency
- Production Rate
- Resource Utilization

---

## 4. Procurement KPIs

Measure:

- Procurement Progress
- Long Lead Item Status
- Vendor Performance
- On-Time Deliveries
- Material Availability

---

## 5. Quality KPIs

Measure:

- Inspection Pass Rate
- NCR Count
- Rework Percentage
- Punch List Status
- Quality Compliance

---

## 6. Safety KPIs

Measure:

- Lost Time Injury Frequency Rate (LTIFR)
- Total Recordable Incident Rate (TRIR)
- Near Miss Reports
- Safety Observations
- Toolbox Talks
- Safety Compliance

---

## 7. Risk KPIs

Measure:

- Open Risks
- High Risks
- Closed Risks
- Risk Exposure
- Mitigation Progress

---

## 8. Executive KPI Summary

Provide:

- Overall Project Score
- KPI Scorecard
- Traffic Light (RAG) Status
- Areas Requiring Attention
- Executive Recommendations

---

# Deliverables

Generate:

- KPI Dashboard
- KPI Scorecard
- Executive Summary
- Performance Analysis
- Corrective Action List

Present results using:
