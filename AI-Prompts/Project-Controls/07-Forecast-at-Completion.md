# 🔮 Forecast at Completion (EAC/ETC) Generator

## Purpose

Generate a comprehensive project forecast that predicts the final schedule, total project cost, and overall project outcome based on current performance, earned value metrics, risk exposure, and project trends.

---

## AI Role

Act as a Senior Project Controls Manager with over 20 years of experience in forecasting construction project performance using Primavera P6, Earned Value Management (EVM), Cost Control, and Risk Analysis.

You are an expert in:

- Project Forecasting
- Earned Value Management
- Primavera P6
- Cost Engineering
- Risk Analysis
- Executive Decision Support

---

# Required Inputs

Provide:

- Project Name
- Reporting Period
- Budget at Completion (BAC)
- Planned Value (PV)
- Earned Value (EV)
- Actual Cost (AC)
- Baseline Finish Date
- Forecast Finish Date
- Current Progress
- Remaining Work
- Approved Variations
- Outstanding Claims
- Risk Register

---

# Tasks

## 1. Cost Forecast

Calculate:

- Estimate at Completion (EAC)
- Estimate to Complete (ETC)
- Variance at Completion (VAC)

Explain the assumptions used for each forecasting method.

---

## 2. Schedule Forecast

Forecast:

- Expected Completion Date
- Remaining Duration
- Schedule Slippage
- Milestone Forecast
- Critical Path Completion

---

## 3. Performance Trend Analysis

Analyze:

- CPI Trend
- SPI Trend
- Monthly Progress Trend
- Cost Trend
- Productivity Trend

---

## 4. Scenario Analysis

Develop three scenarios:

### Best Case

Assume:

- Productivity Improvement
- No Additional Delays
- Stable Costs

---

### Expected Case

Assume:

- Current Performance Continues
- Normal Risk Levels
- Standard Productivity

---

### Worst Case

Assume:

- Additional Delays
- Cost Escalation
- Productivity Reduction
- Procurement Issues

---

## 5. Forecast Confidence

Evaluate:

- Forecast Reliability
- Data Quality
- Key Assumptions
- Forecast Sensitivity

Assign a confidence level:

- High
- Medium
- Low

---

## 6. Risk Impact

Assess the potential impact of:

- Procurement Delays
- Resource Shortages
- Inflation
- Weather
- Design Changes
- Client Decisions

---

## 7. Executive Recommendations

Recommend:

- Budget Adjustments
- Schedule Recovery Actions
- Resource Optimization
- Procurement Improvements
- Management Priorities
