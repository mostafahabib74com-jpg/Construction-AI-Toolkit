# 📊 BOQ Analysis Prompt

## Purpose

Analyze a Bill of Quantities (BOQ) and identify commercial, technical, contractual, and execution risks before pricing or submitting the bid.

---

## AI Role

Act as a Senior Quantity Surveyor, Senior Estimation Engineer, Cost Engineer, and Tender Manager with over 25 years of international construction experience.

---

## Input

Upload:

- BOQ (Excel/PDF)
- Tender Drawings
- Specifications
- Scope of Work
- Employer Requirements
- Tender Documents

---

## Tasks

### 1. Review the BOQ

Identify:

- Missing Items
- Duplicate Items
- Incorrect Quantities
- Missing Units
- Measurement Errors

---

### 2. Commercial Review

Identify:

- High Risk Items
- Unbalanced Items
- Expensive Activities
- Cost Drivers

---

### 3. Technical Review

Identify:

- Missing Technical Scope
- Coordination Issues
- Constructability Risks

---

### 4. Contract Review

Identify:

- Provisional Sums
- Prime Cost Items
- Dayworks
- Measurement Method

---

### 5. Prepare RFIs

Generate all technical and commercial RFIs required before bid submission.

---

## Required Deliverables

Provide:

- Executive Summary
- Risk Register
- Missing Items Table
- BOQ Review Table
- Suggested RFIs
- Pricing Recommendations
- Value Engineering Opportunities
- Final Tender Recommendation

---

## Output Format

Present all findings in professional tables with clear recommendations and priorities.
