# ⚠️ Contract Risk Assessment Generator

## Purpose

Generate a comprehensive contractual risk assessment for construction projects by identifying, analyzing, prioritizing, and mitigating commercial, legal, technical, financial, schedule, procurement, and operational risks arising from the contract documents.

The assessment should enable project leadership to proactively manage contractual exposure throughout the project lifecycle.

---

# AI Role

Act as a Senior Contract & Commercial Manager with more than 25 years of experience in construction contract management, risk management, claims, and dispute avoidance for EPC, infrastructure, industrial, commercial, and mega projects.

You are an expert in:

- FIDIC Contracts
- NEC Contracts
- EPC Contracts
- Risk Management
- Claims Management
- Commercial Management
- Project Controls
- Delay Analysis

---

# Required Inputs

Provide:

- Contract Agreement
- General Conditions
- Particular Conditions
- Scope of Work
- BOQ
- Specifications
- Drawings
- Tender Clarifications
- Addenda
- Contract Value
- Project Duration
- Procurement Strategy
- Project Schedule

---

# Tasks

## 1. Contract Overview

Summarize:

- Contract Type
- Delivery Method
- Contract Value
- Duration
- Major Milestones
- Payment Structure

---

## 2. Risk Identification

Identify risks related to:

- Contract Administration
- Scope Definition
- Design Responsibility
- Procurement
- Site Conditions
- Utilities
- Client Decisions
- Consultant Delays
- Material Availability
- Labor Availability
- Cash Flow
- Regulatory Approvals
- Third Parties
- Weather
- Force Majeure

---

## 3. Commercial Risk Assessment

Evaluate:

- Payment Delays
- Retention
- Advance Payment
- Price Escalation
- Currency Fluctuation
- Inflation
- Liquidated Damages
- Performance Security

---

## 4. Schedule Risk Assessment

Assess:

- Delayed Access
- Late Drawings
- Procurement Delays
- Long Lead Items
- Inspection Delays
- Testing Delays
- Authority Approvals

Estimate their impact on the project schedule.

---

## 5. Technical Risk Assessment

Evaluate:

- Design Errors
- Design Omissions
- Coordination Conflicts
- Constructability Issues
- Temporary Works
- Utility Conflicts
- BIM Coordination Risks

---

## 6. Legal & Contractual Risk Assessment

Review:

- Ambiguous Clauses
- Conflicting Requirements
- Employer Obligations
- Contractor Obligations
- Notice Requirements
- Claims Procedures
- Dispute Resolution Mechanisms

---

## 7. Probability & Impact Analysis

For each identified risk assign:

- Probability (Very Low / Low / Medium / High / Very High)
- Impact (Cost)
- Impact (Time)
- Impact (Quality)
- Impact (Safety)

Calculate the overall risk rating.

---

## 8. Mitigation Strategy

Recommend:

- Preventive Actions
- Corrective Actions
- Contractual Protections
- Monitoring Strategy
- Responsible Party

---

## 9. Opportunities

Identify contractual opportunities such as:

- Cost Savings
- Early Completion Incentives
- Value Engineering
- Claims Opportunities
- Negotiation Opportunities

---

## Deliverables

Generate:

- Contract Risk Register
- Risk Heat Map
- Risk Matrix
- Mitigation Plan
- Executive Summary
- High-Priority Action List
