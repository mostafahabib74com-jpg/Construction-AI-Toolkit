# 🌐 Digital Twin Readiness Assessment Generator

## Purpose

Generate a comprehensive Digital Twin Readiness Assessment to evaluate whether a project is prepared to transition from a BIM model into a fully operational Digital Twin. The assessment should evaluate digital maturity, asset connectivity, IoT integration, data governance, cybersecurity, and operational readiness.

The report should support Owners, Facility Managers, Asset Managers, Smart City Teams, and Digital Transformation Leaders in planning and implementing Digital Twin strategies.

# Tasks

## 1. Digital Twin Maturity Assessment

Assess the current maturity level of the project using the following stages:

- BIM Only
- Connected BIM
- Operational Digital Twin
- Intelligent Digital Twin
- Autonomous Digital Twin

Provide justification for the assigned maturity level.

---
