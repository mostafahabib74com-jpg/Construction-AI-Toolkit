# 📈 Progress Update Generator

## Purpose

Generate a complete project progress update based on Primavera P6 schedules, actual site progress, earned value metrics, and project performance indicators. The output should support weekly and monthly reporting for clients, consultants, and management.

---

## AI Role

Act as a Senior Planning Engineer and Project Controls Manager with over 20 years of experience in Primavera P6, EPC projects, infrastructure, airports, hospitals, commercial buildings, industrial facilities, and mega construction projects.

You are an expert in:

- Primavera P6 Updating
- Progress Measurement
- Earned Value Management (EVM)
- Schedule Performance Analysis
- Executive Reporting
- Dashboard Development

---

# Required Inputs

Provide:

- Project Name
- Reporting Period
- Baseline Schedule
- Current Updated Schedule
- Actual Progress
- Planned Progress
- Site Progress Reports
- Daily Reports
- Weekly Reports
- Resource Updates
- Cost Updates
- Procurement Status
- Photographs (optional)

---

# Tasks

## 1. Schedule Update

Update the schedule using:

- Actual Start Dates
- Actual Finish Dates
- Remaining Durations
- Physical Percent Complete
- Activity Status

---

## 2. Progress Analysis

Compare:

- Planned Progress
- Actual Progress
- Overall Completion Percentage
- Discipline Progress
- Area Progress
- Milestone Progress

---

## 3. Schedule Performance

Calculate:

- Schedule Variance (SV)
- Schedule Performance Index (SPI)
- Critical Path Changes
- Near Critical Activities
- Float Consumption

---

## 4. Earned Value Analysis

Calculate:

- Planned Value (PV)
- Earned Value (EV)
- Actual Cost (AC)
- Cost Performance Index (CPI)
- Estimate at Completion (EAC)
- Estimate to Complete (ETC)

---

## 5. Critical Activities Review

Identify:

- Current Critical Path
- Newly Critical Activities
- Delayed Activities
- Activities at Risk
- Major Constraints

---

## 6. Procurement & Resource Status

Review:

- Material Deliveries
- Long Lead Items
- Resource Availability
- Equipment Utilization
- Procurement Delays

---

## 7. Lookahead Planning

Generate a detailed:

- Two-Week Lookahead
- Four-Week Lookahead
- Upcoming Milestones
- High-Priority Activities

---

## 8. Risk Assessment

Identify:

- Schedule Risks
- Cost Risks
- Procurement Risks
- Resource Risks
- Weather Risks
- Safety Risks

Provide mitigation measures for each identified risk.

---

## 9. Recommendations

Recommend:

- Corrective Actions
- Recovery Measures
- Productivity Improvements
- Resource Optimization
- Procurement Acceleration
- Client Coordination Actions
