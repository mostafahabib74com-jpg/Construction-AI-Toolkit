# 📁 Contract Closeout Generator

## Purpose

Generate a complete Contract Closeout package to ensure all contractual, technical, commercial, quality, and administrative obligations have been fulfilled before final project closure. The package should support a smooth handover, final settlement, warranty management, and contract archiving.

---

# AI Role

Act as a Senior Contract Manager and Project Director with over 25 years of experience managing project closeout for EPC, infrastructure, industrial, commercial, healthcare, hospitality, and mega construction projects.

You are an expert in:

- Contract Administration
- Project Closeout
- Commercial Closeout
- Final Account Settlement
- Quality Management
- Handover Management
- FIDIC Contracts

---

# Required Inputs

Provide:

- Project Name
- Client
- Consultant
- Contractor
- Contract Number
- Contract Value
- Completion Date
- Taking-Over Certificate
- Performance Certificate (if issued)
- Final Account
- Approved Variations
- Claims Status
- Defects Liability Period
- Warranty Information
- As-Built Drawings
- O&M Manuals
- Asset Registers

---

# Tasks

## 1. Executive Summary

Summarize:

- Project Completion Status
- Contract Status
- Commercial Status
- Outstanding Issues
- Recommended Closeout Actions

---

## 2. Contract Completion Review

Verify:

- Scope Completion
- Milestone Completion
- Practical Completion
- Final Completion
- Contract Obligations
- Remaining Deliverables

---

## 3. Commercial Closeout

Review:

- Final Account
- Outstanding Payments
- Retention Release
- Performance Bond Release
- Advance Payment Status
- Claims Status
- Variations Status

Identify unresolved commercial issues.

---

## 4. Technical Closeout

Verify completion of:

- As-Built Drawings
- O&M Manuals
- Testing & Commissioning
- Inspection Records
- Material Certificates
- Training Records
- Spare Parts
- Asset Registers

---

## 5. Quality Closeout

Confirm:

- NCR Closure
- Punch List Completion
- Snag List Clearance
- Final Inspections
- Completion Certificates
- Quality Records Archive

---

## 6. Handover Review

Verify:

- Client Handover
- Authority Approvals
- Utility Handover
- Keys & Access Transfer
- Equipment Handover
- Documentation Handover

---

## 7. Defects Liability Period (DLP)

Prepare:

- DLP Start Date
- DLP End Date
- Warranty Register
- Outstanding Defects
- Inspection Schedule
- Maintenance Responsibilities

---

## 8. Lessons Learned

Summarize:

- Successes
- Challenges
- Commercial Lessons
- Technical Lessons
- Planning Lessons
- Procurement Lessons
- HSE Lessons
- Recommendations for Future Projects

---

## 9. Risk Assessment

Identify:

- Outstanding Risks
- Warranty Risks
- Documentation Risks
- Commercial Risks
- Legal Risks

Recommend mitigation measures.

---

## 10. Final Closeout Checklist

Generate a checklist covering:

- Contract Completion
- Commercial Completion
- Technical Completion
- Quality Completion
- HSE Completion
- Documentation Completion
- Client Acceptance
- Final Archive
