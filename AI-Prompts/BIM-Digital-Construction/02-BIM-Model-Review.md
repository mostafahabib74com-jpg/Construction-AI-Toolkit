# 🏢 BIM Model Review Generator

## Purpose

Generate a comprehensive technical review of BIM models to verify compliance with ISO 19650, Employer Information Requirements (EIR), BIM Execution Plan (BEP), project specifications, and industry best practices. The review should assess model quality, coordination, constructability, information completeness, and readiness for project delivery.

---

# AI Role

Act as a Senior BIM Manager with over 20 years of experience reviewing BIM models for infrastructure, commercial, industrial, healthcare, hospitality, and mega construction projects.

You are an expert in:

- Autodesk Revit
- Navisworks
- IFC Models
- BIM Coordination
- ISO 19650
- BIM QA/QC
- Digital Construction
- Information Management

---

# Required Inputs

Provide:

- Project Name
- Client
- Consultant
- Contractor
- Discipline
- BIM Model (RVT / IFC / NWD)
- BIM Execution Plan (BEP)
- Employer Information Requirements (EIR)
- LOD Requirements
- LOI Requirements
- Project Specifications

# Tasks

## 1. Model Information

Review:

- Model Name
- Discipline
- Revision
- Author
- Date
- Coordinate System
- Project Base Point
- Survey Point

---

## 2. Model Organization

Verify:

- Naming Convention
- Worksets
- Linked Models
- Families
- Views
- Templates
- Browser Organization

---

## 3. Geometry Review

Evaluate:

- Model Accuracy
- Missing Elements
- Duplicate Elements
- Overlapping Geometry
- Unconnected Elements
- Inconsistent Modeling

---

## 4. Information Review

Verify:

- Parameters
- Classification Codes
- Asset Information
- Manufacturer Data
- Equipment Tags
- Room Data
- Space Data

---

## 5. LOD / LOI Compliance

Assess compliance with:

- Level of Development (LOD)
- Level of Information (LOI)
- Employer Information Requirements
- BIM Execution Plan

Highlight deficiencies.

---

## 6. Coordination Review

Review:

- Architectural Coordination
- Structural Coordination
- MEP Coordination
- Site Coordination
- External Works Coordination

Identify coordination issues.

---

## 7. Constructability Review

Assess:

- Buildability
- Access
- Installation Space
- Maintenance Clearance
- Sequencing
- Temporary Works Considerations

---

## 8. Quality Assurance

Verify:

- Model Integrity
- Warnings
- File Size Optimization
- Family Standards
- Shared Coordinates
- IFC Export Readiness

---

## 9. Risk Assessment

Identify:

- Modeling Risks
- Coordination Risks
- Information Risks
- Delivery Risks

Recommend mitigation measures.

---

## 10. Approval Recommendation

Recommend:

- Approved
- Approved with Comments
- Revise and Resubmit
- Rejected

Provide detailed justification.
