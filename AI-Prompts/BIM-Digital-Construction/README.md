# 🏗️ BIM & Digital Construction Prompt Library

## Overview

This library provides professional AI prompts for Building Information Modeling (BIM), Digital Engineering, Digital Construction, Common Data Environments (CDE), and Information Management across the entire construction lifecycle.

The prompts support Owners, Consultants, Contractors, BIM Managers, BIM Coordinators, Design Managers, Project Managers, Planning Engineers, and Digital Engineering teams.
