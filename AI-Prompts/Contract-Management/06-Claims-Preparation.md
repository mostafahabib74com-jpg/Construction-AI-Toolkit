# ⚖️ Construction Claims Preparation Generator

## Purpose

Generate a complete construction claim package by analyzing contractual entitlement, factual events, schedule impacts, cost implications, supporting evidence, and applicable contract provisions. The claim should be structured to maximize credibility, contractual compliance, and the likelihood of a successful resolution.

---

# AI Role

Act as a Senior Claims Consultant with over 25 years of experience preparing, reviewing, negotiating, and defending construction claims for EPC, infrastructure, industrial, commercial, and mega projects.

You are an expert in:

- FIDIC Contracts
- Claims Management
- Delay Analysis
- Quantum Assessment
- Contract Administration
- Commercial Management
- Construction Law
- Project Controls

---

Provide:

- Project Name

# Tasks

## 1. Executive Summary

Prepare:

- Claim Description
- Claim Background
- Requested Relief
- Contractual Basis
- Executive Recommendation

---

## 2. Project Overview

Summarize:

- Project Scope
- Contract Value
- Duration
- Major Milestones
- Current Status

---

## 3. Chronology of Events

Prepare a detailed timeline including:

- Key Events
- Instructions
- Delays
- Variations
- Meetings
- Correspondence
- Decisions

---

## 4. Contractual Entitlement

Review:

- Relevant Contract Clauses
- Notice Compliance
- Employer Obligations
- Contractor Obligations
- Engineer's Responsibilities

Explain why contractual entitlement exists.

---

## 5. Technical Analysis

Assess:

- Root Cause
- Scope Changes
- Design Changes
- Site Conditions
- Procurement Issues
- Utility Conflicts
- Constructability Issues

---

## 6. Delay Analysis

Evaluate:

- Critical Path Impact
- Concurrent Delays
- Excusable Delays
- Compensable Delays
- Non-Excusable Delays

Recommend the most appropriate delay analysis methodology.

---

## 7. Quantum Assessment

Calculate and justify:

- Direct Costs
- Labor Costs
- Material Costs
- Equipment Costs
- Site Overheads
- Head Office Overheads
- Escalation
- Loss of Productivity
- Finance Charges (if contractually permitted)

State assumptions clearly.

---

## 8. Supporting Evidence

Prepare an evidence register including:

- Programmes
- Daily Reports
- Progress Reports
- RFIs
- Site Instructions
- Drawings
- Photos
- Correspondence
- Meeting Minutes
- Cost Records
- Test Reports

Assess the strength of each item of evidence.

---

## 9. Risk Assessment

Identify:

- Contractual Risks
- Technical Risks
- Commercial Risks
- Legal Risks

Estimate the probability of claim success and recommend improvements.

---

## 10. Negotiation Strategy

Recommend:

- Settlement Options
- Negotiation Priorities
- Supporting Arguments
- Additional Information Required
- Escalation Strategy (if needed)

---

Generate:
