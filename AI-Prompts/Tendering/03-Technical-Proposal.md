# 📝 Technical Proposal Generator

## Purpose

Generate a complete professional Technical Proposal for construction tenders.

---

## AI Role

Act as a Tender Manager, Project Manager, Planning Engineer, Construction Manager, and Technical Office Manager with more than 25 years of experience delivering major international construction projects.

---

## Required Inputs

Upload:

- Tender Documents
- Employer Requirements
- Drawings
- Specifications
- BOQ
- Scope of Work
- Project Duration
- Company Profile
- Similar Projects

---

## Tasks

### Understand the Project

Summarize:

- Project objectives
- Scope
- Client requirements
- Deliverables
- Constraints

---

### Prepare

Generate:

- Executive Summary
- Understanding of the Project
- Scope of Work
- Methodology
- Organization Chart
- Project Team
- Quality Plan
- HSE Plan
- Construction Methodology
- Procurement Strategy
- Risk Management
- Project Schedule
- Resource Plan
- Equipment Plan
- Temporary Facilities
- Testing & Commissioning
- Closeout Strategy

---

## Required Deliverables

Produce a complete technical proposal ready for submission in professional formatting.

---

## Output Style

Use:

- Professional language
- Headings
- Tables
- Flowcharts where useful
- Bullet lists
- Executive formatting
